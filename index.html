Description


Lead your cat on a stress-free walk with the PetSafe Easy Walk Harness. When your cat pulls, the Easy Walk Harness uses gentle pressure on the chest and shoulder blades to steer your dog to the side and refocus his attention on you. The chest strap rests low on the breastbone instead of the throat to eliminate choking, coughing and gagging.

- Discourages your cat from pulling, lunging and jumping while on leash
- Low chest strap prevents choking, coughing and gagging
- 4 adjustment points provide maximum comfort and reliable fit
- Made from soft, but strong, nylon; Quick snap buckles located on belly straps for easy on and off
- Includes detailed training and fitting guide; Complementary color on belly strap to easily identify top and bottom
